Welcome to Syllabee!

Syllabee is an interactive syllabus platform that allows you to navigate a syllabus piece by piece. Generally, a syllabus provides you with a blueprint for a course and often serves as a contract between you and your instructor. Within you're likely to find the course description, the course goals, topics covered, course schedule, grade determination, textbook information, and more. Your instructor has compiled all this information, which will be presented to you one section at a time.

Navigation is easy; click the button below to get started. On subsequent blocks, you'll also have a Previous button if you need to go back and review anything (as long as your instructor has enabled this option). At any time, you can use the Table of Contents on the right (click the button in the top right corner if you are on mobile) to quickly navigate to any previously accessed blocks (again as long as your instructor has enabled this option). You'll also have the option to save or print your syllabus once you've visited all blocks (this option will be available directly beneath your instructor's contact information on the Table of Contents).

If you experience any issues, feel free to contact your instructor using one of the contact methods at the top of the Table of Contents.
